Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report an issue?

Simply visit the "Report Issue" page, fill out the form with details about the problem, and include a photo if possible. Your report will be reviewed and assigned to the appropriate department.

How long does it take to resolve issues?

Resolution times vary depending on the type and severity of the issue. Emergency issues are prioritized and typically addressed within 24‚Äì48 hours, while routine maintenance may take several weeks.

Can I track my reported issue?

Yes! All issues are publicly visible on the map and issue list. You can see the current status, which department it's assigned to, and upvote issues to show community support.

Is this service free to use?

Absolutely! CivicReport is a free public service designed to improve communication between citizens and local government. No registration or fees are required to report issues.
